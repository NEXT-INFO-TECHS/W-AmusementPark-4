For every 10 student tickets purchased, you will receive one complimentary ticket for a chaperone. Additional chaperone tickets should be purchased in advance of your trip and will be eligible for the group rate. Chaperones must be 21 years of age or older.

Buses will be directed to park along 34th Street, south of the entrance to Tiger Lot. Buses park for free, and bus drivers are admitted to the Zoo for free with proper ID. Anyone traveling in their own vehicle will need to pay for parking in any of our open lots.

You are permitted to bring your own lunches and eat freely at any of our available picnic areas, no reservations required. Please note that we do not have storage for lunches. You may leave and re-enter the Zoo if you choose to store lunches on your bus.

We are not currently offering guided group tours.

Please check our calendar for available dates. Please note our discounted school rate is available for grades K-12 during our weekday operating hours only.
